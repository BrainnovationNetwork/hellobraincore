Why

Everyone gets a brain. Nobody gets the manual.

Your brain decides how you sleep, what you remember, and how today feels. Most of us were never taught one useful thing about taking care of it. That's the whole reason this show exists. Each episode is a real conversation with someone who knows the science, and we stay on the question until the answer makes sense.

For the curious

You like knowing how things work, and this particular thing happens to be running your life.

For the caregiver

Someone you love has a changing brain. You want to understand what's happening and what actually helps.

For the professional

You work in healthcare and want the science kept straight, current, and worth your commute.
